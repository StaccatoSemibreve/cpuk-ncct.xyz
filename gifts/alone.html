Alone

"So, how'd it feel!?"

"Aaa!!! Could you not sneak up on me while I'm birdwatching!?"

"Oh, sorry!" Peppermint said unapologetically, smiling widely and speaking in a singsong tone. "How'd it feel?" she whispered.

"How... how'd what feel?" asked Crimsonaut, fearing he may already know the answer.

"Having him inside you!" she exclaimed, gesturing wildly. "Your helpless body filled with his very essence as he uses you as he pleases-"

"Okay, please stop, that's enough, I get the picture!"

"I could go on if you'd like~!"

"I can assure you I would not. At all. Anyway, it... it's a painful memory, but I can talk about it. Besides, it's been on my mind lately, and I haven't had anyone to talk about it with in a long time.

It was... weird, but the weirdest thing was that it wasn't that weird??? Like, before the Crimson showed up, I couldn't have imagined the experience for a second. But after he was a part of me, and I was a part of us, it was completely natural?

It wasn't my body any more, it was our body. Sometimes I'd be in control, sometimes I'd be in the passenger's seat, sometimes I'd be asleep, but even when he... even when we fought, it felt like this was where he belonged, right here with me.

Having him look from my eyes, listen through my ears, walk on my legs, fight with my hands, talk out of my mouth, never felt wrong, it just felt like I wasn't currently the one in control of our body. I've talked to the others about it, and everyone else felt... violated, but I never did... I just felt like Crimson was where he belonged."

"With meee!!!"

"Sure. But since he's been gone, I've been... not alone, of course, I have the Crimsonlets and they're just wonderful, but in here," he poked at his head. "In here I've been alone.

I want to hate him for what he did as me, to me, for resenting me, for betraying me. I want to move on, like I can live my life alone again, I don't want to miss him, but it's so empty in here all by myself! I want him-"

"Me too!!!"

"I want him to co- to return to me, to apologise, but most of all just to stay this time. I know he hated this body, which honestly i can't blame him for, but I don't think this body is built for one. I don't... I don't want to be alone in here any more..."

"Fascinating~! I was hoping it was a sex thing, but your answer's good too! Bye nowww~~~!"

"Goodbye Peppermint......"

As she walked away, he retrieved his binoculars. He could almost imagine Crimson's voice snarking about how bird watching was a waste of time, time that could be spent making plans or failing to build a tolerance to cheese. He could... almost, hear him. But he couldn't really hear him, and hadn't been able to for a long time now.

He sighed, and returned to watching the- oooh, is that a cardinal!?

"Oooh, is that a cardinal!?" Peppermint shouted in the distance, scaring the bird away. Yes, Peppermint, yes it was.
